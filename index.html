Our Open Source Operating System.

In development, written in Python. Read More

We're FREEproject.

We're a passionate group of people who believe that open source is the way to go.

We're creating an operating system, based on the popular Linux distro Debian, that has Python as its interface. The code that we're implementing is fresh and up-to-date, providing you with the newest features and security updates that will keep your system in perfect form. Turn your old computer into a new one with our OS, or even put our OS on a USB stick. We're even considering on putting the OS on Raspberry Pis that will have GPIO capabilites. The future's exciting!

Keep up-to-date about our OS on Google+ or learn more on our OS page.
